      Health Tips

      Simple habits for a healthier hostel life

      Stay Hydrated

      Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily. Set reminders if needed.

      Sleep Well

      Aim for 7-8 hours of sleep. Avoid screens before bed.

      Eat Clean

      Avoid mess food that looks or smells off. When in doubt, skip it.

      First Aid Kit

      Keep a basic kit: band-aids, antiseptic, paracetamol, ORS, thermometer.

      Wash Hands

      Wash hands before eating and after using the bathroom.

      Mental Health

      Feeling stressed? Talk to friends, take breaks, exercise.

      Water Intake Guide

      Aim for 8-10 glasses of water daily. Here's when to drink:

      • **Morning:** 2 glasses after waking up
      • **Before meals:** 1 glass 30 mins before eating
      • **Study breaks:** 1 glass every hour
      • **Night:** 1 glass before bed (optional)
